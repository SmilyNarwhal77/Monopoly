What is Monopoly?

Monopoly is a board game where players buy and sell properties, build houses and hotels, and collect rent from their opponents. The goal is to be the last player left in the game.
How to play:

  • Players roll two dice to move around the board.
  • Players can buy properties if they aren't already owned by another player.
  • Players can build houses and hotels on their properties.
  • Players can collect rent from their opponents if they land on one of their properties.
  • Players can use Chance and Community Chest cards to change their fortune.
  • Players can bankrupt their opponents to win.
