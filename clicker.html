Clicker games are video games where players perform repetitive actions, like clicking, to earn rewards. They are also known as incremental games, idle games, or tap games.

How do clicker games work?

  • Players click on the screen to accumulate resources, like currency
  • Players can use the currency to upgrade characters or items, and unlock new features
  • Players can progress in the game even when they are not actively playing
  • In some games, players can progress without having to perform any actions

Why are clicker games popular?

  • Their simple mechanisms make them popular
  • They are easy to pick up and play
  • They encourage a sense of accomplishment, with clear goals and checkpoints
  • They encourage a sense of growth as players upgrade

Examples of clicker games: Cookie Clicker, Clicker Heroes, and Capybara Clicker.
Other clicker game subgenres:

  • Idle games that blend with other game types, like business simulations and world-building adventures
  • Combat-based idle games, like Tap Titans 2
